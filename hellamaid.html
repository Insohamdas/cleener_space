Before and After Scenes of our Work!

Empowered Cleaners, Happier Clients

At our cleaning service, we prioritize the happiness of our cleaners above all else because we believe that their well-being directly translates into the satisfaction of our clients. When our cleaners are happy, they feel secure, appreciated, and respected – and that’s our number one goal. Their happiness creates a positive ripple effect, leading to delighted clients who continue to choose our services.

Redefining Cleaning Services with Integrity and Innovation

Established in 2017, Hellamaid is on a mission to change the cleaning industry. Led by Ahmed and Abdul (two engineers), Hellamaid strives to make cleaning services a better experience for both ends of the market: homeowners and cleaners. We offer value to homeowners through easy online booking and impeccable customer service, while offering a flexible, respected, and well paid opportunity to our cleaning partners. As certified members of the Ontario Living Wage, BC Living Wage, and Alberta Living Wage initiatives, and a proud supporter of the Canadian Cancer Society, we take pride in our dedication to supporting our cleaners as well as various causes and charities throughout Canada.

Driven by a Passionate Team

Behind our exceptional cleaning services stands a dedicated 15-member office team, committed to supporting both our cleaners and clients every day. Their passion ensures that our cleaners have the tools and support they need, while our clients receive the top-notch service they deserve. Want to know more about our story?
